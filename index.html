Welcome to our Mars Operation

In order to make the operation works fine, we need you to drive our rover around planet Mars.

The rover stars on the following position with north direction (col5, line 5):

You can choose a sequence of the following commands to move the rover:

  1. "f" to go forward
  2. "b" to go back
  3. "r" change the direction right*
  4. "l" change the direction left*

*Note that if you select "r" or "l" the rover won't move right or left automatically. For example, if the Rover have direction "N" and we send the command r then the new direction of the Rover will be E.

Important notes:

  • The rover is on a 10*10 grid.
  • You can go from one edge of the grid to another.
  • The grid has obstacles and you can not cross them.
  • You can check the "map" of the grid on the console, it refresh with each movement.
